Contents Cleaning

When the unexpected happens in your home or business, the team at Recovery Solutions is here to get you back on your feet with our residential and commercial contents cleaning services.

Whether you’ve experienced a fire, flood or other disaster, our 24/7 response team is ready to help you clean and restore your damaged items so that you can get back to your everyday life as soon as possible. We clean and restore any items, including: electronics, appliances, furniture and clothing.

Read Post

Dealing with smoke, soot, water and other contaminants can be tricky and a fast response is crucial. If you take too long or use the wrong cleaning method, you could risk permanently damaging items that could’ve been saved. Trust our experienced cleaning and restoration team to increase your chances of a high success rate and have peace of mind knowing your items are in good hands.

Using a combination of cleaning and decontamination procedures, our technicians will work diligently to stabilize your belongings as soon as possible. This will help to slow corrosion, staining and the spread of contamination.

Depending on the level of humidity, temperature and contaminants involved, your items could have a higher chance of staining or corroding. To prevent this, we place your property in a decontamination chamber, reducing moisture and minimizing the effects of contaminants.

Whether you’re dealing with a post-fire or -flood disaster, your items may develop a smell that can be hard to remove by a simple wash. In addition to helping with moisture reduction, our decontamination chamber will help to deodorize your belongings.
